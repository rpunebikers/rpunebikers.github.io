About PuneBikers

Our Story

Born on Pune's roads. Raised by adventure. Built by brotherhood.

Mission

Why We Ride

It started with a Reddit post, a 300 km Konkan ride, and a stranger's comment that changed everything. Three people who had never met planned a ride in a comment section, showed up, rode together, and posted photos. That was January 2023 — and the spark that lit r/PuneBikers.

Our mission is to build a community where every rider, regardless of experience, feels welcome, safe, and inspired to explore further than they ever thought possible.

We organise rides, promote responsible motorcycling, mentor new riders, and create memories that last a lifetime.

Philosophy

How We Ride

🛡️

Safety Is Not Optional

Full gear is mandatory on all group rides. No exceptions. We believe arriving is more important than speed.

🤝

No Rider Left Behind

We ride as a unit. Pace is set by the group, not the fastest rider. Every pit stop waits for the last bike.

🌿

Respect the Road

Leave every place better than you found it. No littering. No reckless riding through villages or wildlife zones.

📖

Always Learning

We run regular riding workshops, first-aid training, and mechanical basics. A better rider is a safer rider.

Safety Culture

Gear Up. Every Time.

Our safety checklist isn't a formality — it's how we make sure everyone comes home.

⛑️

Full-Face Helmet

ISI/DOT certified required

🧥

🥊
