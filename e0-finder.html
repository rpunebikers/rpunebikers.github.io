Find E0 Fuel

Most petrol in India is now E20 — blended with up to 20% ethanol. Ethanol absorbs moisture and can corrode fuel lines, gaskets and carburettors, especially on older and high-performance bikes, and it slightly lowers mileage. Use the finder below to locate stations selling E0 (ethanol-free) petrol before your next ride.
