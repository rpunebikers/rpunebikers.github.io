Roads Worth Riding

Curated routes by PuneBikers — tested, rated, and ready for your next adventure.

Western Ghats

The Home Range

The Sahyadri mountain range — our backyard and the most beautiful riding terrain on earth.

🌿

Tamhini Loop

Easy

Pune → Tamhini → Mulshi → Pune

The classic Pune day loop. Waterfall-lined ghat roads, chai stops, and back for dinner. Perfect for new riders.

📏 160 km ⏱ 5–6 hrs ⛽ 2 fuel stops
🌄

Mahabaleshwar Run

Easy

Pune → Mahabaleshwar → Panchgani

Table-top plateau viewpoints, strawberry farms, and crisp mountain air. A timeless route.

📏 240 km ⏱ 7–8 hrs ⛽ 2 fuel stops
🏕️

Kas Plateau Circuit

Moderate

Pune → Kas → Thoseghar → Satara

UNESCO World Heritage wildflower plateau. Best in Sept–Oct. Stunning views at Thoseghar waterfalls.

📏 280 km ⏱ 9 hrs ⛽ 3 fuel stops

Coastal Roads

Salt & Speed

Where the Western Ghats meet the Arabian Sea — India's most scenic coastal highways.

🌊

Konkan Coast NH66

Easy

Pune → Mumbai → Alibaug → Ratnagiri → Goa

India's most scenic coastal highway. Sea views, seafood shacks, and smooth tarmac. 3-day classic.

📏 680 km ⏱ 3 days ⛽ Multiple
🏖️

Alibaug Day Trip

Easy

Pune → Khopoli → Alibaug Beach

Cross the Ghats and hit the coast. Alibaug is perfect for a sunrise–sunset day ride.

📏 160 km ⏱ 5 hrs ⛽ 1 fuel stop
🚢

Tarkarli & Sindhudurg

Moderate

Pune → Kankavli → Tarkarli → Malvan

Clear blue waters, sea forts, and Malvani cuisine. Two days of coastal bliss.

📏 430 km ⏱ 2 days ⛽ 3 fuel stops

Mountain Rides

Into the Himalayas

For riders ready to push boundaries. These routes demand preparation, experience, and respect.

🏔️

Ladakh Circuit

Expert

Delhi → Manali → Leh → Srinagar → Delhi

The ultimate Indian motorcycle odyssey. High-altitude passes, remote valleys, and roads that test every rider.

📏 2,400 km ⏱ 14–16 days ⛽ Plan carefully
🌨️

Spiti Valley

Expert

Shimla → Kaza → Manali Loop

The moonscape valley with Buddhist monasteries perched on cliffs. Open only June–September. Unforgettable.

📏 1,100 km ⏱ 10 days ⛽ Limited availability
🌲

Uttarakhand Chardham

Moderate

Haridwar → Kedarnath → Badrinath → Gangotri

Sacred mountains and pilgrim roads. Technically moderate but spiritually epic.

📏 1,600 km ⏱ 12 days ⛽ 6+ stops

Lonavala Overnight

Easy

Pune → Lonavala → Old Mumbai Highway

Nashik Wine & Ride
